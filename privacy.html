Introduction

Welcome to FamilyWall. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

FamilyWall collects the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account
  • Profile Photo: Optional profile picture you upload
  • Content: Lists, tasks, events, notes, messages, and photos you create within the app
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you use the app
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to help us fix bugs and improve stability

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the FamilyWall service
  • To sync your data across your devices
  • To send push notifications about family activity
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns
  • To provide customer support

Data Sharing

Your data is shared only in these circumstances:

  • Family Members: Content you create is visible to members of your family group
  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication, database, and file storage
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud). We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. Access to your data is restricted to family members you have invited.

Push Notifications

FamilyWall may send push notifications to inform you about family activity, such as new list items, completed tasks, and chat messages. You can disable notifications at any time in your device Settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the app
  • Edit or update your profile information
  • Delete your account and all associated data (Settings → Account → Delete Account)
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting support

Children's Privacy

FamilyWall is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of FamilyWall after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
